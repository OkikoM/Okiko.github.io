Learning Consultant & Coach

Over 10 years building and delivering learning experiences for both physical and distributed environments

Interested?

I am continuously fascinated by how the mind works.

How do humans process data from the environment and use it to make decisions? What actions can we take to improve the performance and efficiency of our minds and translate this to our personal and professional lives?

My curiosity and expertise have led me through a career in adult learning in both the academic and commercial sectors. I have designed and delivered learning programmes for University Students, Professors, Bankers and Software Engineers in multiple African cities. Throughout it all, I've held to one prime directive: to get learning right.

Currently, I'm serving as Global Coordinator for Growth and Development at Andela, helping to build the future of talent, software, and product development across Africa, North America, and Europe.
