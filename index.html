Learning Consultant

10+ yrs Building and Delivering Learning Experiences in both Physical and Distributed Environments

Interested?

I am fascinated by how the mind works.

How can people best process the information around them to make the most impactful decisions?

Are there any actions we can take to deliberately improve the performance and efficiency of our minds and translate this to our personal and professional lives?

My curiosity and expertise have led me through a career in adult learning in both the academic and commercial sectors. I have designed and delivered learning programmes for University Students, Professors, Bankers and Software Engineers in multiple African cities. Throughout it all, I've held to one clear aim: to get adult learning right.

Currently, I'm serving as Global Coordinator for Growth and Development at Andela, helping to build the future of talent, software, and product development across Africa, North America, and Europe.
